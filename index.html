bangalore

NAMMA BANGALORE

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It has a population of more than 8 million and a metropolitan population of around 11 million!

MYSURU

MYSURU

Mysore is noted for its heritage structures and palaces, including the Mysuru Palace, and for the festivities that take place during the Dasara festival when the city receives hundreds of thousands of tourists from around the world. It lends its name to various art forms and culture, !

gokarna

GOKARNA

Gokarna has a vast sea line to offer to the visitors. They are calm and serene amid the waters, hills and sunlight. Gokarna Main Beach Source The famous trio of beaches in Gokarna, the Kudle Beach, The Half-moon Beach and the Paradise beach are the most famous ones that have brought in international recognition!

HAMPI

HAMPI

Hampi or Hampe, also referred to as the Group of Monuments at Hampi, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in east-central Karnataka, India. Hampi was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century. Chronicles left by Persian and European travellers, particularly the Portuguese, say that Hampi was a prosperous, wealthy and grand city near the Tungabhadra River!

MANGALORE

MANGALORE

Mangalore is famous for its Udupi style dishes and ice creams along with the staple seafood preparations. Visitors must try out the Neer Dosa, Mangalorean fish curry, Cashew Upkari, Rasam, Kane (Lady Fish), Ole Bella (Palm Jaggery), export quality cashews and coffee in Mangalore.
